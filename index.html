We are art enthusiasts and providers of art enrichment to children with special needs in Fulton, Cobb, and Paulding Counties.
We believe everyone has an artist inside looking to express themselves,
and we want to help your child find their artistic voice.

At CogniDoodle it is our mission to offer a service that many children with special needs do not have the opportunity to try.  We strive to enhance and strengthen cognitive and language skills while students learn new ways to express themselves.

Our primary focus is on process-based art enrichment where the value of creating art is the experience, not the finished product. This allows students to have full creative freedom.

Everyone on our staff has experience working with children with exceptionalities. We offer art enrichment classes and social skill groups for children through our visual arts and language-based curriculum. Visual art experiences support children by growing skills such as critical thinking, self-expression, problem-solving, communication, and collaboration.

ABOUT OUR FOUNDERS

Our founders Tristyn and Laurelle are proud mothers to loving, caring, talented, and creative kids. They each have a child who was made different, not less . Fusing each founders' education and passion, CogniDoodle aims to offer more programming for families with special needs children.

Tristyn has 9 years of experience providing support in schools and clinical settings. She has a background in speech and language pathology. She is currently pursuing her Master's in Counseling. In these settings, she served as a member of the Assistive Technology Committee and Coordinator for her school’s Autism Support Team. Tristyn spends her free time baking with her son, gardening, crafting, and advocating for families with special needs.

Laurelle is a professional illustrator and designer. She has 7 years of experience as an art instructor to seniors and children. She has a background in Science and Fine Arts. She also has experience working in elementary schools teaching Art, STEM, and Robotics. Her specialties include colored pencil and acrylic media. Laurelle enjoys coloring with her children, working as a makeup artist, painting, crocheting, and volunteering as a Girl Scout troop co-leader.
