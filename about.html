What is CogniDoodle?

CogniDoodle is an inclusion program (a setting where children with special needs work together with typical peers) that aims to strengthen and develop cognitive, language, and social skills through art. We offer process-based art enrichment where self-expression is the goal. This allows all students to improve their critical-thinking and communication skills without worrying about their artistic talent.

Why we do it?

Well, we were just like you. "What do you mean?" I'm glad you asked.
We were looking for programs and activities that would be fun and functional for our kiddos. Something that would expose them to new experiences while being inclusive. In our search, we kept coming across programs that were great but also exclusive‚Äîseparating children with disabilities and typical children. However, research shows that there are benefits for children with and without disabilities in inclusion programs. We couldn't find what we were seeking. So, we created our own.
