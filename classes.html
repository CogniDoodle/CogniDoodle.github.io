We offer several options for parents to enroll their little ones with CogniDoodle.

Peer Connect

Here your child will learn new skills and make new friends. Using the CogniDoodle curriculum, our instructors focus on building social interaction and communication skills.

Individual Lessons

We will tailor the lessons to meet personal needs and interests of the student.

Host Us

We bring CogniDoodle classes to your location. We provide our CogniDoodle curriculum, a variety of creative materials, and a CogniDoodle trained teacher. You provide adequate space for us to offer the classes.
