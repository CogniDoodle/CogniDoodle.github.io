Making our vision reality.

Established in 2017, CogniDoodle aims to offer more programming for families with children, no matter their needs.  We are unbiased and operate under the premise that all people are created differently, and we can help each other succeed.  There are no limitations or preconceived notions for anyone involved.

Our Team

We can't make change alone. Nor are we afraid to admit it. Our team is comprised of compassionate people who enjoy working with children. They all have experience working with kids and are trained by CogniDoodle.

OUR VALUES

We are all different—not less. We know this and believe this.

People first.

Each and every student is an individual. They have their own personalities and quirks. Everyone deserves respect and fair treatment.

Differences are not limitations.

We hear the words "can't" and "never" associated with children too many times. With patience and compassion, we can overcome challenges as long as we are dedicated.

Exclusivity is for snobs.

We aren't selling a luxury product or holding up entry lines here. Inclusion boosts self-confidence, fosters communication, and increases cognitive skills.
