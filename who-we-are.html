Making our vision reality.

Established in 2017, CogniDoodle aims to offer more programming for families with children, no matter their needs.  We are unbiased and operate under the premise that all people are created differently and we can help each other succeed.  There are no limitations or preconceived notions for anyone involved.

Our Team

We can't make change alone.  Nor are we afriad to admit it.  Our team is comprised of compassionate and caring people who enjoy working with children.  They all have experience working with kids and are trained by CogniDoodle.

Why we do it?

Well, we were just like you.  "What do you mean?"  I'm glad you asked.
We were looking for programs and activities that would be fun and functional for our kiddos.  Something that would expose them to new experiences while being inclusive.  In our, search we kept coming across programs that were great but also exclusive—separating children with disabilities and typical children.  However, research shows that there are benefits for children with and without disabilities in inclusion programs. We couldn't find what we were seeking.  So being good moms, we created our own.

So, Who's behind this?

Our founders Tristyn and Laurelle, of course.  Established in 2017, CogniDoodle was created by the proud moms to loving, caring, talented, and creative kids.
